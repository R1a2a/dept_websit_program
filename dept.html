Department Overview

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The Department of Computer Applications was established in the year 2001.
The department offers two programmes:

1.Master of Computer Applications (MCA) â€“ 2 years (4 semesters)
2.PhD (Computer Applications) under Research Centre affiliated by VTU.

The MCA programme focuses on training students in the fields of Application Software Development, Enterprise Resource Planning,
Mobile development, Data Mining & Warehousing, Machine Learning etc.
The programme also nurtures competent professionals through real-world projects, thereby and fosters continuous learning.
Accreditation, Approvals and Recognition:
The Department of Computer Applications is approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), and is affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU),
Belgaum in Karnataka. CMRIT is also one of the handful of college awarded NAAC A+ grade in the year 2017 and the Department was accredidated by NBA in 2011.

vesion

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

To evolve as a center of excellence in computing education producing globally competent professionals who can contribute for the betterment of society.

Mission

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  • M1: To prepare students for a global career in computing by enriching the curriculum with a blend of theory and practice.
  • M2: To build a faculty team with industry and academic exposure to nurture competent professionals.
  • M3: To engage with industry and institute of repute and to collaborate in academics, research, development and consultancy involving faculty and student.
  • M4: To envisage and implement real world project and foster continuous learning amongst students through life-skill, industry relevant programming and internship.

Program Outcome

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Computational Knowledge: Ability to apply knowledge of mathematics, Computing fundamentals and specialization.

1.Problem Analysis: Ability to identify, formulate and analyze complex computing Problems.

2.Design /Development of Solutions: Ability to design, solve and evaluate solution for complex computing problems.

3.Conduct investigations of complex Computing problems: Ability to conduct systematic investigations of systems and data during design & development to derive valid conclusion.

4.Modern Tool Usage: Ability to use the techniques, skills, and modern tools necessary for complex computing techniques.

5.Professional Ethics: Ability to apply and commit professional ethics and cyber regulations in a global economic environment.

6.Life-long Learning: Ability to engage in independent learning for continual development with proactive measures.

7.Ethics: Project management and finance: Ability to understand financial and management principle in multidisciplinary environment.

8.Communication Efficacy: Ability to comprehend and write effective reports, design documentation and make effective presentation.

9.Societal and Environmental Concern: Ability to analyze the global and local impact of business solutions on individuals, organizations and the society.

10.Individual and Team Work: Ability to act as a member or leader in diverse teams in multidisciplinary environments.

11.Innovation and Entrepreneurship: Ability to use creativity and entrepreneurial vision to create value and wealth for betterment of individual and society at large.
